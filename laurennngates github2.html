    About Me

    Hi, I'm Lauren

    I'm a music enthusiast who loves to attend live shows. I started this website to share my experiences with others. My favorite genres to see live are rock, pop, and indie mixes of the two. I am very new to this and currently just playing around making this website. I hope you can find some good recommendations and enjoy!
